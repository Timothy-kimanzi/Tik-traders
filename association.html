Tik traders sacco rules

Membership Eligibility:

For one to be a members should register with 1000 shillings and be ready to pay 200 per year for renewal.

Membership Rights and Privileges:

Once a member you can get any service in our sacco. Your savings are always safe and ready for withdrawal.

Savings Requirements:

Every one is encouranged to make as much savings as possible, the more savings you make the higher loan limit you create

Loan Eligibility and Criteria:

Every member as a right to get a loan, provided he/she has made savings. Loan limit is calculated using the amount you have saved. You can get a loan of twice the amount you have saved.

Loan Application Process:

Apart from savings there is no limit(unless blocked officials due to inconveniences) for any member.

Loan Limits:

The limit of your loan is twice the savings you have made.

Interest Rates:

Interest rates are conducive and affordable to our members, they are given in loans page.

Repayment Terms:

You have to pay the previous loan before acquiring another loan.

Savings and Investment Products:

In order to increase the support to our members we help them advertise their product in our webpage. we also organise a marry go round for the interested members.

Dividend Policy:

Savings will be growing at a rate of 6% per month

Member Education and Training:

Offer educational programs and training sessions to empower members with financial literacy and management skills.

Governance Structure:

Our sacco is led by a board of directors who are chosen amongest our member by our members for a period of 3 years.

Code of Conduct:

In order to be a competitive sacco in the society we need our members, officials and any participant to use higher rate of conduct othere wise there will be a penalty.

Complaints and Dispute Resolution:

Any dispute is solvable, report it to the right department.

Membership Termination:

if any member wants to terminate his Membership he/she will be required to visit the office clearance. He/she will get a shre of 75% of his/her savings

Annual General Meeting (AGM):

A mandatory annual meeting will be hard ater every 3 years for all members failier to attend a penalty will be given.

Amendment Procedures:

Sacco rule will be amended by the board of directors.For long term rules will require our members to vote for them(4 months and above)
