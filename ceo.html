Life of a CEO: JAMES NJAGI

Introduction

James is the CEO of TIK TRADERS SACCO, a leading SACCO in the country and outside the country. Born and raised in Gacigongo, James showed exceptional leadership qualities from a young age.


Early Life

James attended Kenyatta University, where he BUSINESS MANAGEMENT. During his college years, he founded his first startup, which laid the foundation for his entrepreneurial journey.

Professional Career

After graduating from KU, James worked at several renowned tech companies, where he gained valuable experience in product development, marketing, and management.

CEO Role

In 2022, James became the CEO of TIK TRADERS SACCO. Under his leadership, TIK SACCO company has achieved remarkable growth and innovation, expanding its reach globally.

Philanthropy

Besides his professional endeavors, James is actively involved in various philanthropic activities, supporting education and healthcare initiatives.

Achievements

Throughout his career, James has received numerous awards and recognitions for his contributions to the development of society at large.
